# Booking Agent Prompt Pack

## Lead research prompt

> Research this venue/festival/community using public information only. Do not scrape private data. Return fit notes, public contact policy, safety concerns, and whether outreach should remain closed.

## Pitch draft prompt

> Draft a short booking email using approved assets only. Do not send. Include a subject, 120-word body, proof links, and a human approval checklist.

## Follow-up prompt

> Draft a respectful follow-up. Do not pressure, do not automate sending, and include a no-contact option.
