# Human Approval Ledger

Default state: `closed_until_human_yes`.

## Decision slots

- `approve_booking_pitch_send`
- `approve_followup_send`
- `approve_calendar_invite`
- `approve_fee_or_offer_language`
- `approve_public_announcement`
- `approve_asset_attachment`

No slot opens without a human approving target, copy, timing, attachments, and risk notes.
